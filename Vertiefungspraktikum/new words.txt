encompass (v): to inclue different types of things

conformation (n): the arrangement formed by the atoms in a molecule when 
		  they move freely around, that can often change

Vitrification (n): the process of changing, or changing something, into
		   glass, or into a substance like glass

parenteral (adj): relating to food or treatment that does not come through
		  the digestive system, for example drugs that are injected
		  into the veins or muscles

surrogate (v): something that replaces or is used instead of something else
